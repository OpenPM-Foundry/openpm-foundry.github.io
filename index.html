    What is OpenPM Foundry?

    OpenPM Foundry is a hub for crafting open-source project management tools and playbooks specifically designed for early-stage organizations. We understand the unique challenges that early-stage teams face, and we're building resources to help you succeed.

    Our playbooks provide actionable guidance for managing tech projects efficiently when resources are limited but ambitions are high.

    Key Objectives

    • Establish best practices for project management in early-stage teams
    • Curate and integrate open-source tools that won't break the bank
    • Foster collaboration within the tech community through shared knowledge
